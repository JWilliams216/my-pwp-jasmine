Content Strategy

My site will be one page about balancing life and mental health, and consist of the following sections

Navigatoin Bar

Navagation bar, sticky to top, title of website and different sections displayed, and (responsive to go to different sections within page)

Section 1/"Introduction"

A short introduction about my website, no interaction

Children

A heading, below will be text describing different things you could do with your children. Two cards displayed side by side, each with a heading (Bedtime and Dancing/Singing). Bedtime will go through a time efficient routine and ways to be engaged. Dancing/Singing will have links to different YouTube videos of songs for kids and dances you could do with your child. Below the cards will be a five tired collapsed accordion, the heading is Meaningful Activities, within the accordion will be different things to do with children that would provide quality time (slime creation, sandbox, mud pies, crafts, etc.). Below will be a list of book images (12) (to read with children), links to free online books when you click the image, below the book images are the title of the books.

Household

A heading, below will be four columns (Schedule, Organizing, Commitments, and Chores). Color coded to keep organized. Picture on top and ul underneath, will match heading of column to help keep eyes focused on correct section. Links on bottom li's to additional resources (chore chart, google calendar, google keep, etc.).

Mental Health & Self Care

Carousel with caption, arrows to click next and previous, clickable lines on bottom to each slide. Six slides, each with a heading (Breaks/Relaxing, Boundaries, Exercise, Emotions/Triggers, Nutrition, and Sleep) and text

Support & Community

Text explaining who in your life could be considered as part of your support "team" and community. Below is a flush accordion with four tiers (Groups, Apps, and Tiplines/Websites), within each tier are four image cards with links to different groups, apps, tiplines, and websites to build their community. The text on card will give some description about site connected (knowledge about different sections available on site linked before they leave my site). Below text giving addition information about what may be needed in order to achieve goal (nutritionist, doctor, therapist).

Footer

Text "I hope this is helpful <3" in the center of a black footer..
