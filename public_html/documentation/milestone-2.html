Content Strategy

My site will be one page providing knowledge about balancing life and mental health, and will consist of the following sections.

Navigation Bar

The navigation bar will contain the title of my site, aligned left on large screens and will be the width of the screen on small screens. Responsive links to the different sections of my site will be displayed to the left on large screens and within a hamburger to the left on small screens.

Introduction

On the left will be an image, on the right will be an introduction to my site and its contents. There will not be any interaction in this section.

Children

In this section I will describe how to spend quality time with children with a time efficient bedtime routine, dancing and singing, meaningful activities, and books. Providing ways to be present and engaged with the time you have. It’ll have a heading, two cards next to each other on large screens, and they’ll stack on small screens, one will contain a list of the bedtime routine and the other will have links to different YouTube videos of songs and dances. Below the card, will be a five tiered collapsed accordion, a heading of “Meaningful Activities”, it will display meaningful activities such as, crafts, slime creation, sandbox, mud lies, zoo, etc., within each drop down will be instructions or links to each activity. Below will be twelve images books in a row with their titles beneath them, the images will have links attached to free online books.

Household

This section will have lists on how to organize commitments, schedule, chores, and a calendar. It’ll contain four columns on a large screen and will be stacked on small screens. Each with a heading, an image displaying an example of the set up, and a list, they’ll be color coded to help keep eyes focused.There will be links on the bottom of each list to additional resources such as Google Calendar, a chore chart, Google Keep, etc..

Mental Health & Self Care

Carousel with caption, arrows to click next and previous, clickable lines on bottom to each slide. Six slides, each with a heading (Breaks/Relaxing, Boundaries, Exercise, Emotions/Triggers, Nutrition, and Sleep) and text

Support & Community

Text explaining who in your life could be considered as part of your support "team" and community. Below is a flush accordion with four tiers (Groups, Apps, and Tiplines/Websites), within each tier are four image cards with links to different groups, apps, tiplines, and websites to build their community. The text on card will give some description about site connected (knowledge about different sections available on site linked before they leave my site). Below text giving addition information about what may be needed in order to achieve goal (nutritionist, doctor, therapist).

Footer

Text "I hope this is helpful <3" in the center of a black footer..

Design

Color scheme to match milestone-1, maybe add a couple colors that compliment existing ones to give myself more a variety so my patterns aren't the same thought site. Maybe breaks between sections with image in background.
